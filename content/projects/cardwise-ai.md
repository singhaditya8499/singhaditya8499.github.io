CardWise AI is a personal-finance assistant focused on helping users evaluate credit-card options, align card selection with financial goals, and understand spending behavior through AI-generated summaries.

## Focus Areas

- Personalized card recommendations based on user goals and constraints.
- Goal-aware planning support for safer and more structured decision-making.
- Natural-language insights to explain spending patterns in simple terms.
