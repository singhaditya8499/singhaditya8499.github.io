Customer Reise is a support-focused web application built to reconstruct and replay customer journeys from logs so teams can diagnose user-reported issues faster and with more confidence.

## Highlights

- Aggregates cross-service logs into a coherent user journey timeline.
- Enables issue replay workflows for support and engineering teams.
- Improves root-cause analysis speed for production incidents.
