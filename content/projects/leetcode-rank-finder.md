LeetCode Rank Finder is a lightweight automation utility that solves the practical challenge of locating a specific contestant quickly in large contest ranking tables.

## Highlights

- Fetches and parses contest ranking data.
- Searches standings by username and returns rank details.
- Reduces manual effort during contest analysis.
